For me and my partners project, we decided to have a small "concert" consisting of a song as well as lights that blinks in sync with the music. We chose "Never Gonna Give You Up" and proceeded from there. We began by finding an online converter to find the notes for the song, and then added them into the program. However, the code that we had did not originally work with the notes, but through trial and error of different lines of code we were able to get it working. After that, we began focusing on the light, which I worked on and struggled with the placement on the board. However, I also used trial and error to solve this, and afterwards my partner added the code and it worked perfectly.

As you can see by the video, our Arduino is programmed to play the song "Never Gonna Give You Up" by Rick Astley, and a blue led blinks in sync with the music.
